Different Types Of Tea

1. Black Tea

  • Black teas are fully fermented. When tea leaves are fully oxidized they become black colour. This tea is the most consumed tea type.

  • Benefits: Black tea improves gut and heart health, Helps to reduce blood pressure, It helps to improve our focus

2. Green Tea

  • Green tea is orginated in China. It has not undergone the process which is used to make Black and Oolong tea. It is unfermented. After the leaf is picked it will be wizened, heated and after rolling it will be dried.

  • Benefits: Green tea helps to improve brain functions, Helps to prevent some types of cancers, Helps to lose weight, It reduces the bad breath

3. Oolong Tea

  • To produce Oolong tea they partially oxidized it unlike others. Because of this process it gets different taste and the color ranging from green to dark brown.It is a Chinese traditional tea.

  • Benefits: It helps to prevent diabetes, Helps to strengthen your tooth and bones, It will also help you to lose your weight

4. White Tea

  • White tea is also known as silver tips. They are harvested before the tea plant's leaves open fully, when the young buds are covered with the white hairs. White tea is not oxidized and it is the least processed among other tea types. It is only withered and dried in a controlled environment.

  • Benefits: White tea reduces the risk of heart diseases, Helps to protect your teeth from bacteria, It will also help you to lose weight
